Flashover Alerts

Watch for these critical indicators in a structural fire environment:

  • Rollover: Isolated flames licking through the smoke layer (Ghosting).
  • High Heat: Intense radiant heat forcing you down to the floor.
  • Black Fire: Thick, turbulent, dark smoke that carries high energy.
  • Lowering Neutral Plane: The smoke layer rapidly descending toward the floor.
  • Sudden Temperature Rise: A rapid, uncontrolled increase in thermal layering.

IMMEDIATE ACTION REQUIRED: VENT OR EXIT
